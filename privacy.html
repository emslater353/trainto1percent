Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 28, 2025

AI Proof Club ("we", "us", "our") helps you build skills that stay valuable as AI changes work. This policy explains what we collect, why, and your choices.

Information we collect

  • Account info: name, email address, and password (stored securely via Supabase Auth).
  • Profile info: industry, role, goals, and work responsibilities you provide during onboarding or in Settings.
  • App activity: mode scores, quests, receipts, streaks, forecast preferences, and progress data.
  • Coach messages: text you send to AI coaches inside the app.
  • Support messages: content you send through our support form.
  • Device info: basic technical data needed to run the app (browser or mobile app version, general usage).

How we use information

  • Provide and personalize quests, forecasts, coaching, and progress tracking.
  • Maintain your account and sync data across sessions.
  • Send account-related emails (welcome, password reset, optional weekly quest emails if you opt in).
  • Improve the product and fix issues.

AI processing

Some features use third-party AI services (including OpenAI) to generate coaching responses, assessments, and reviews. Content you submit in those features may be processed by those providers according to their policies. Do not submit sensitive personal information you do not want processed for this purpose.

Third-party services

  • Supabase — authentication and database hosting.
  • Netlify — app hosting and backend functions.
  • OpenAI — AI coaching and analysis features.
  • Resend — transactional email delivery.
  • Beehiiv — optional newsletter if you subscribe.
  • Stripe — web subscription billing (not used for in-app purchases on iOS).

Data retention

We keep your data while your account is active. You can delete your account at any time in Settings → Delete Account. When you delete your account, we remove your profile and associated app data from our systems.

Your choices

  • Update profile information in Settings.
  • Opt out of marketing/newsletter emails using unsubscribe links.
  • Delete your account in the app (Settings → Delete Account).
  • Contact us with privacy questions at support@aiproof.club.

Children

AI Proof Club is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. We will post the updated version on this page with a new "Last updated" date.
