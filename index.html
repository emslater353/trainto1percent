How is this different from reading forecasts online?

Anyone can read predictions. We map the business opportunities inside each forecast — and train the skills you need to act on them. Knowing the future is useless if you can't move on it.

What are the three modes?

Ideas = strategic thinking, frameworks, decision-making. Personality = influence, communication, social calibration. Execution = shipping, follow-through, action-taking. AI Proof Club can switch between all three.

How much time does this take?

15 minutes per day — but it's not "extra" time. The quests happen inside your existing day: meetings, emails, conversations you're already having.

Can you really train personality or social skills?

You're not changing who you are. You're sharpening how you communicate, read rooms, and respond under pressure. Timing, clarity, even adding levity — these are trainable.

What if I'm already strong in one mode?

Good. The diagnostic finds your specific gaps. Maybe you're great at ideas but avoid execution. Maybe you ship but stay invisible. Everyone has underdeveloped modes.

What if it doesn't work for me?

The system works if you do the quests. If you're not completing challenges, you're not training. The diagnostic is free — start there and see if this is for you.
